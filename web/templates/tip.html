Neural Cryptography

This is a web application to perform Steganography , the practice of hiding data in data, by hiding text in images, images in images, and video in video. The steganographic algorithms used were learned by large, Convolutional Neural Networks . This work was done as a part of a final project for an MIT class, 6.857: Computer and Network Security.
